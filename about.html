Our Story

Janju Diner began as a family dream inspired by a passion for food and togetherness. The mother of the owner loved selling homemade goods, from fermented delicacies and sweets to hearty Filipino meals. When the samgyupsal trend began booming after the pandemic, the family saw an opportunity to bring a unique dining experience to their local community.
